Luciola

Luciola Latin for Fireflies
The motif is the sparkle of fireflies, and the object
of the furniture is baby furniture.
We will breathe life into abandoned furniture to protect
the environment and provide a warm and safe space for babies.

Product Introduction

Rocking Cradle - An “upcycled furniture” piece, transformed from
an original chair into a rocking cradle.

  • Materials Used: Birch plywood, nails, natural fabric, cushion
  • Reused Materials: Parts from the original chair
  • Safety: Rounded edges designed with child safety in mind
  • Design: Warm colors and a modern style to suit parents' aesthetic sensibilities
  • Dimensions (L x W x H): 865 x 415 x 438 (mm)
